ABOUT OOS

ABOUT

“FUNK MUSIC THRIVES IN THE SILENCE BETWEEN NOTES, WHERE THE MAGIC OF THE GROOVE IS TRULY BORN

& THE SHOW IS AT THE

CENTRE OF THE HEART.”

Funk is what you don’t play! The band understand that funk is not just about the notes played but the spaces left between them—these pauses breathe life into their music, making each performance vibrantly alive.

Since their first show, Out Of Season have worked hard to give their audience the best performance possible. For Out Of Season, lights, special moments on stage, and funky tricks are key to making their shows special. The band do everything they can to make sure every show is an unforgettable experience that touches everyone who comes to see them.

THE BACKBONE OF THE BAND

All of the members of Out Of Season have a deep passion for groovy music with roots in the last century’s old-school soul and funk.

LIVE PERFOMANCES

Touring DK with gigs in Sweden, Luxembourg and France, the band has been around and has proven its live quality for various audiences. They can connect well with many different audiences, showing that their music can be enjoyed by people in many places. This shows their hard work and how much people like their music wherever they go.

YEARS OF FUNK EXPERIENCE

Formed in 1990, the band boasts a deeply experienced rhythm and horn section as its backbone. This seasoned ensemble of musicians has grown tightly knit over the years, has become the hallmark of their identity, enabling them to deliver performances that resonate with both precision and soul. This shared musical journey has become a defining characteristic of the band, setting a solid foundation for their distinctive style and sound.
