OUT OF SEASON BEGAN IN 1990

Out Of Season is a Copenhagen-based funk band established in 1990. The band performs a mix of its own compositions and interprets covers. Out Of Season is still active and has played over 250 concerts, mainly in Copenhagen, with tours around Denmark and Sweden. The music is inspired by the World’s most unique voices of funk, soul and acid jazz – everything from Mr Thing and the Professional Human Beings to Tower Of Power, Maceo Parker, Defunkt, James Brown and more.
